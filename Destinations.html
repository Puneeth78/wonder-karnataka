1.Coorg (Scotland of India)
  • Famous for coffee plantations, misty hills, trekking trails, and river rafting.
  • A paradise for nature lovers and adventure seekers.
2.Chikkamagaluru
  • Known for trekking spots like Mullayanagiri (highest peak of Karnataka).
  • Coffee estates, waterfalls, and peaceful hill stays.
3.Gokarna
  • Beautiful beaches (Om Beach, Kudle Beach, Paradise Beach).
  • Perfect for adventure water sports and camping by the sea.
4.Hampi (UNESCO World Heritage Site)
  • Ancient ruins, temples, and boulder landscapes.
  • A mix of culture, history, and adventure (rock climbing).
5.Bandipur & Nagarhole National Parks
  • Wildlife safari experiences with tigers, elephants, and deer.
  • A must for nature and photography enthusiasts.
6.Jog Falls
  • One of the highest waterfalls in India.
  • A breathtaking view, especially in monsoon season.
7.Mysuru
  • Rich in heritage with Mysore Palace, Chamundi Hills.
  • Blends history, tradition, and modern Karnataka.
8.Dandeli
  • Adventure capital with river rafting, kayaking, jungle camping.
  • Wildlife spotting in dense forests.
